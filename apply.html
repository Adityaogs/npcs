Whitelist Application

Follow these steps to apply for ReaperSMP whitelist.

  1. Join our Discord using the invite link on the site.
  2. Introduce yourself in #introductions and show activity.
  3. Read the rules in #rules and confirm you accept them.
  4. Fill the small application in #apply (format: IGN, age, timezone, why you want to join).
  5. Be patient — staff review and whitelist usually within 48–72 hours for active applicants.
