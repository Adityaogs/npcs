Server Rules

Clear, fair, and enforced with community safety in mind.

  1. Be respectful — zero tolerance for harassment, hate, or discrimination.
  2. No cheating — any client/cheat that gives unfair advantage is banned.
  3. English required in public channels for moderation clarity.
  4. No griefing outside of designated PvP zones or agreed wars.
  5. Do not share personal info or dox other players.
  6. Abide by staff decisions; appeals may be presented in #ban-appeals.

Consequences

Violations can lead to warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.

  • 1st: Warning
  • 2nd: Temporary Ban
  • Severe: Permanent Ban
